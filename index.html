Free Range

We are a software development co-operative, based in London.

Small by design, we are developing software with a focus on reputation, sustainability, quality and happiness.

What sets us apart

Free Range isn't a typical company, because we have no CEO or top-down structure. We work as colleagues and peers, which means that we're all directly involved in the development of our software.

We've chosen this structure because it allows us to collaborate with some of the best developers in the world. Unlike more typical companies we have almost no overhead costs, which means we can focus entirely on delivering
