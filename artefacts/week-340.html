Friday 24th July, 2015

by Chris Roos

Week 340

Afternoon y’all.

It’s really rather rainy here in London today. I imagine it’ll just last the day and then we’ll be back to a blazing hot Summer again. Probably.

It’s been an incredibly short week this week. James has been on holiday and I’ve had a couple of days off for a wedding (good times) and a funeral (sad times).

I spent Tuesday and Thursday working on Smart Answers, and today’s been spent catching up on GFR things.

Smart Answers

  • Tadas did a great job fixing a couple of errors I’d managed to introduce when switching to ERB templates for childcare-costs-for-tax-credits and calculate-state-pension. We’re really trying to avoid impacting Tadas’s work so it was a bit disappointing that these problems managed to slip through the net.

  • I merged my branch to switch maternity-paternity-calculator to use ERB templates.

  • I merged my branch to add support for indented ERB templates.

  • I indented the outcome templates for 31 of 35 Smart Answers now that it’s possible.

  • I had my change to add the page option to our Analytics tracking accepted in the govuk_frontend_toolkit.

GFR

  • Organised Show and Tell 15. Sign up to Show and Tell mailing list if you’re interested in hearing about these upcoming events.

  • Published week links for week 340.

  • Chris L spent the afternoon here and published Web Audio Weekly 44.

Until next time.

– Chris
