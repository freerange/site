Week 904
========

[James](/james-mead) is on leave in the Isles of Scilly.
It's been on my list to visit for quite a few years but I'm sadly yet to make it.
Keeping everything crossed the weather is kind while James is there ☀️ .

[Chris](/chris-lowis) and I spent the week working on the [Manage Vaccinations in Schools project](https://github.com/NHSDigital/manage-vaccinations-in-schools/) (Mavis).
Our focus continues to be on performance but I also spent a while trying to get a handle on our end to end tests.
They've become increasingly flakey over the past few weeks and I think there's a risk that we end up ignoring them if we can't trust the results.
As a team we've also been dealing with the fallout of the [repos being made private](https://www.theregister.com/software/2026/05/05/nhs-to-close-source-github-repos-over-ai-security-concerns/5224392), including but not limited to:

- CI breaking because of permission issues
- CI runners being smaller for private projects and therefore jobs taking longer to run
- Release notes not being public
- Team members losing visibility of the repos

We spent an enjoyable day in the Good Machine office on Wednesday.

Until next time.

-- Chris
