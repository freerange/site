Week 906
========

This was another short week due to the late May bank holiday.
[James](/james-mead) returned from his travels to the Isles of Scilly while [Chris](/chris-lowis) headed off to the Wye valley for a week with the family.
It's half term so I've only worked part time while juggling childcare.

James and I have continued working on the performance improvements that Chris and I started last week.
We're working to break apart some long running Sidekiq jobs into much smaller jobs that can benefit from horizontal scaling.
We're making progress but it's been a bit slow going as we found a few other things that needed fixing along the way, and are still dealing with a flakey end to end test suite.

Today has been about catching up on email, messages and GFR admin that I've been neglecting for a while.
I'm hoping that by the end of today I'll feel a bit more on top of things...

Until next time.

-- Chris
